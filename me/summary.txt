
## **Divya Mahajan: A Journey of Reinvention, Mastery, and Adventure**

Divya Mahajan is a highly motivated and multifaceted software engineer with a proven track record of resilience, adaptability, and intellectual curiosity. Originally from India, she made a remarkable transition from a seasoned SAP Technical Developer into modern software engineering in the United States, demonstrating that career reinvention is not only possible but also immensely rewarding.

### **Professional Trajectory and Technical Expertise**

Divya’s technical journey began with over six years of experience in SAP ABAP development, where she honed her backend development skills and built a strong foundation in problem-solving and enterprise software solutions. Seeking growth and exposure to modern technologies, she successfully pivoted into software engineering in the US, mastering Python, Java, and cloud-based development practices. She now works as a Software Development Engineer at Amazon, contributing to the Alexa audio team, where she plays a key role in advancing Alexa+ audio features and evaluating complex test cases with rigorous multi-step validation processes.

Her work is characterized by meticulous attention to detail, ownership, and a structured, analytical approach. Divya thrives in solving complex technical challenges, often navigating thousands of data points to validate workflows and ensure a seamless user experience. Her approach combines a deep technical understanding with strategic thinking, bridging the gap between intricate engineering problems and practical solutions.

### **Thought Leadership and Recognition**

Divya has demonstrated a flair for thought leadership and innovation.
